Wellington House Group

Branding | Web design & Development

I was hired by Wellington House Group who provide tree care and landscaping services. They required a whole brand to be created as well as an online presence. This meant I was responsible for designing a logo, business cards, flyers, website design and development. I chose to build this site in WordPress to utilize various plugins.

Branding

After doing a lot of research on competitor tree care & landscaping companies, I noticed a lot of inconsistencies throughout competitors branding. Studying BA Media Communications at University, I learnt that it’s imperative your brands identity stays consistent through out all communication channels.

The logo was created in Affinity designer in two color ways. One to sit on the darker backgrounds i.e. dark green and black. The other to sit on lighter shades like white and grey. The font I chose is called Sansita. I liked its italic and friendly nature which I personally think helps the brand look like a premium service.

Instagram feed

The owner of the company wanted to build a social media presence and thought a good way for him to do this was to upload images and interesting videos of various projects. As he isn’t the most tech savvy person, I added an Instagram feed to his website, so every image he posts on Instagram it automatically feeds to the website.

Icons

I noticed a lot of tree care and landscaping companies just listed their services in plain text. I thought it would be good to visually represent the various services with icons. Not only is it visually more appealing, it will help the reader identify what service they require without having to think too much.
